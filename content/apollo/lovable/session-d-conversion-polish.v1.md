1. **Numbers over adjectives.** *"180kg deadlift at thirty-nine"* beats *"got stronger fast."*

4. **"Without [the thing gurus do]" construction.** Position by subtraction.

- Zero em dashes (—) in any customer-facing string.
- Zero semicolons. Zero mid-sentence colons.
- One exclamation mark allowed in *"FINALLY"* if it appears in body copy. Never on a primary CTA.
