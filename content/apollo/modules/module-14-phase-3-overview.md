### Phase 3 Overview

You have spent sixty days in the forge.

The foundation is built. Your training is consistent. Your nutrition is dialled in. Recovery is no longer an afterthought, it is a system. You have proven that you can show up, execute, and sustain.

Now we refine.

Phase 3 is where we take the machine you have built and optimise the details that separate a strong man from an exceptional one. The way you stand. The way you breathe. The way your body actually absorbs and utilises the food you eat. The way you sleep, not just the duration, but the architecture of it.

These are the things most men will never think about. And that is exactly why they plateau.

But we do not leave gaps.

Four modules. Each one goes deep. Each one changes something fundamental about how your body operates.

Lets begin.
