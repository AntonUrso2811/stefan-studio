Hard rules

01

Studio, partner, or practice — never "AI agency."

02

One ox-blood mark per asset. Default is Ink on Bone.

03

Two typefaces per asset. Mono only for data.

04

No gradients · shadows · emoji · hashtags · stock imagery · rounded corners above 2 px.

05

UK English throughout. £ before the number, no decimals on round figures.

06

Locked hero tagline — "AI operations for DTC founders, by an ex-DTC founder."

07

Refused vocabulary: transformation · synergy · unlock · seamless · leverage (v.) · game-changer · cutting-edge · revolutionary · AI-powered.

08

Banned CTAs: "Click here" · "Learn more" · "Get started" · "Sign up" · "Submit". Replace with a specific action naming a specific outcome.
