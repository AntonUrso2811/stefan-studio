Urso & Co 04 · Palette
§   Four values. No tints. Ink on Bone, always.
Ink#0f1114Primary. Wordmark, body, headings. Warm near-black, never pure #000.
Bone#f6f2ebSecondary. Default page ground. Warm off-white, paper register.
Ox-blood#5b1e24Accent. One placement per asset. Ink dried on paper.
Stone#c4bdb4Utility. Dividers, captions on Ink. Never text on Bone.

Urso & Co 10 · Hard rules
§   Eight things no one on the team gets to break.
01

Studio, partner, or practice — never "AI agency."

02

One ox-blood mark per asset. Default composition Ink on Bone.

03

Two typefaces per asset. Plex Mono only for data.

04

No gradients · shadows · emoji · hashtags · stock imagery.

05

UK English throughout. £ before the number, no decimals on round figures.

06

Locked hero tagline. Do not paraphrase above the fold.

07

Refused vocabulary is refused. No exceptions. No tonally-justified workarounds.

08

Banned CTAs. Never "Click here", "Learn more", "Get started", "Sign up", "Submit".
